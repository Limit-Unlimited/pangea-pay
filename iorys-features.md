### Customers
* Blacklist requests: user has to the option to blacklist a customers
* Customer register: a full register of customers. Full customer profile displays the basic information, shareholders, UBO, additional users, risk assessments, etc.
* Info: All communication is tracked for each customer, including emails. Any documents exchanged via email are also visible on the app. The email tracking can also track if the email was read by the customer or not.
* Current Accounts: list of all current accounts held by customers, their status, balance, open/close/block date, etc.
* Account rights: customername, username, language, status, is business owner user, etc.
* Reminders: list of any reminders to review ay customer account/profile, HR: 6 months, MR: 18 months, LR: 24 months
* Users: a customer can have multiple users, this is the list of the additional user per account, with their permission level
* Sanctions List
* Commissions
* SAR reports: Internal and External. 
* Partner deal list

### Configuration
* System Configuration: All system related configurations
* Products: Manage all products: ID, Productcode, productname, productgroup, businesname, status
* Pricelist: list of all charges for each product. The price is variable based on customers' risk category
* AddInfo configruation: field configuration
* Reference Rates: list of all historical currency rates in the platform
* Currency configuration: configure all currencies, country, currency, decimal value, status, FX margin
* Country configuration: code, name, EU, OECD, IBAN, Country risk, Restrictions, Main currency, etc.
